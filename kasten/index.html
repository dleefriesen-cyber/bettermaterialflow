The System

Everything reconsidered.
Nothing compromised.

01

Hidden Assembly

Lamello P-System connectors are machined into every panel at our facility. No visible screws. No exposed hardware. The result is a seamless, furniture-grade finish that traditional closet companies cannot replicate.

02

Precision at Scale

Every module begins on a CNC machining center. Tolerances are held to fractions of a millimeter. Shelf pin holes, connector pockets, and hardware prep are machined before a single edge is banded — guaranteeing perfect alignment every time.

03

Flat-Pack Logistics

Kasten ships flat and assembles fast. Each module is sequenced, labeled, and packed with pre-sorted hardware kits. A complete walk-in closet arrives in a fraction of the space — and installs in a fraction of the time.

04

Modular by Design

Four widths. Three depths. Infinite combinations. The Kasten module matrix generates any closet configuration from a small family of precision components — reducing manufacturing complexity while maximizing design flexibility.

05

Adjustable Foundation

Häfele Axilo adjustable legs provide a clean, elevated base with concealed leveling. Height adjustments happen from above — no getting on the floor, no visible feet. A toekick panel conceals everything for a built-in appearance.

06

Integrated Lighting

Häfele Loox LED lighting integrates seamlessly into the system via a concealed bus-bar. A single power connection illuminates the entire closet — no individual wire runs, no visible cords, no afterthought fixtures.

The Install

A complete walk-in.
In a single morning.

01
Layout & Level

Mark module centerlines with a laser level. Locate structural support. Set Axilo legs to finished floor height. 15 minutes.

02
Connect Modules

Clamex P14 connectors click panels together and lock with a quarter-turn. No glue, no nails, no exposed fasteners. 30 minutes.

03
Install Interior

Shelves drop onto System 32 shelf pins. Drawers mount on pre-drilled undermount slides. Accessories click into place. 20 minutes.

04
Doors & Lighting

Salice soft-close hinges are pre-mounted from the factory. Plug the Loox bus-bar into a single outlet. The closet is done. 15 minutes.

The Engineering

Built on the same connector system used by European furniture makers.

Kasten is built around the Lamello P-System — a precision connector technology used in high-end European cabinetry. Every connector pocket is CNC-machined before panels leave our facility, guaranteeing perfect alignment and a hidden joint that is both stronger and cleaner than traditional fasteners.

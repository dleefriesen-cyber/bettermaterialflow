Form submissions are stored in a Google Sheets spreadsheet and imported into our CRM. Data is transmitted securely over HTTPS. Google's infrastructure is used for storage — you can review Google's privacy practices at policies.google.com/privacy.

05

Analytics and Advertising

This site uses Google Tag Manager to manage analytics and advertising tags, including:

  • Google Analytics — to understand how visitors use the site (page views, traffic sources)
  • Google Ads — to measure whether ad campaigns lead to demo requests

These services may set cookies and collect anonymized usage data. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking via Google's opt-out tool.

06

This site uses Cloudflare Turnstile to protect the contact form from automated spam. Turnstile verifies that submissions come from real users without displaying a CAPTCHA puzzle or tracking your browsing behavior. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy and the Turnstile Privacy Addendum for details.

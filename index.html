Questions Worth Asking About Your Shop

Most workflow problems in small cabinet shops are handling problems — not machining problems.

01

How many times does a sheet get touched, moved, or repositioned before it becomes a finished cabinet part?

02

How much of your floor space is dedicated to staging and processing sheets — and how much is left for actual production?

03

How often are sheets damaged, chipped, or scratched during handling — and what does that cost in material and time?

04

How much labor in your shop is spent moving material from point A to point B instead of machining or assembling it?
